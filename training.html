Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be fit to join a training program?

No, our programs are designed for all fitness levels. Our trainers will help you get started and progress at your own pace.

What should I bring to my first class?

Bring comfortable workout clothes, a water bottle, and a towel. If you are attending a yoga class, you may want to bring your own mat.
